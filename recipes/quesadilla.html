Quesadilla Recipe

Ingredients

  • Shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Tortilla
  • Avocado oil or Olive oil

Step-by-step Recipe

  1. Turn stove to medium heat
  2. Place flat pan on stove
  3. Oil up the pan with your choice of Avocado oil or olive oil
  4. Put Tortilla on pan
  5. Put Cheddar on Tortilla
  6. Use a spatula to flip the quesadilla when needed
  7. Cook both sides to desired crispiness
