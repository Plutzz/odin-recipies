Shaved Ice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Garlic Oil
  • Bazil
  • Whatever toppings you would like!

Step-by-step Recipe

  1. Make dough
  2. Kneed dough until smooth
  3. Place dough in a well-oiled bowl and cover with a damp cloth
  4. Set aside to rise until doubled, about 1 hour. Punch down; knead in garlic and basil. Set aside to rise for 1 more hour, or until doubled again
  5. Preheat your grill for high heat
  6. Carefully place one piece of dough on the hot grill. Dough will begin to puff almost immediately. When the bottom crust has lightly browned, turn dough over using two spatulas.
  7. Top with each of the following: tomato sauce cheese, basil, and whatever other toppings you want!
  8. Close the lid and cook until cheese melts. Remove from grill and set aside to cool for a few minutes
